How it works

Listen the stories of our members about what they learnt after joining the community.

Meeting Format

Club has regular meetings once in a week for 1.5 hours where you can practice publiс speaking skills. Every meeting has the same structured format. It consists of 3 main parts:

Prepared Speeches

Members deliver from five to seven minute prepared speeches followed by instructions

  • learn from educational manuals
  • choose your topic for the speech
  • deliver the speech in your club

Impromptu Session

Speakers have up to 2 minutes to answer an unexpected question

  • learn how formulate answers quickly
  • practice organized responses
  • master out-of-the box thinking

Evaluation

You will get feedback on your speech as well as time management, grammar mistakes

  • know how to improve your speech
  • get feedback from Grammarian
  • get support and feedback from your clubmates

How you will master your skills

Educational system Pathways will help you to build the competencies you need to communicate and become a better leader

1

Become a member

As a guest you can visit club meetings and take part in impromptu session, but to deliver projects you need to be a member of a club.

Find your club to join, the payment is ~$8 per month. Club’s officers will explain you all the procedure.

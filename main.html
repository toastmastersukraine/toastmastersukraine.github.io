FAQ

short answers on what interests you most

Can I come and just listen?

Sure! You might even surprise yourself as our sessions are so engaging you might want to get on stage.

Is it a languages courses? Do you have teachers?

No, we are a community where you learn by doing. Every time you are on stage, you get new experience; at the end you get a feedback on your performance.

Who will evaluate me than?

A more experienced member of the club.

What the difference between guests and members?

As a guest you can visit any Toastmasters club for free, countless times. Only enjoying the limited participation - impromptu sessions or taking small roles. As a member you get access to all educational materials, get a mentor and start delivering your speeches.

How it works

Listen the stories of our members about what they learnt after joining the community.

Meeting Format

Club has regular meetings once in a week for 1.5 hours where you can practice publiс speaking skills. Every meeting has the same structured format. It consists of 3 main parts:

Prepared Speeches

Members deliver from five to seven minute prepared speeches followed by instruction

  • learn from educational manuals
  • choose your topic for the speech
  • deliver the speech in your club

Impromptu Session

Speakers have up to 2 minutes to answer an unexpected question

  • learn how formulate answers quickly
  • practice organized responses
  • master out-of-the box thinking

Evaluation

You will get feedback on your speech as well as time management, grammar mistakes

  • know how to improve your speech
  • get feedback from Grammarian
  • get support and feedback from your clubmates

Become a member

As a guest you can visit club meetings and take part in impromptu session, but to deliver projects you need to be a member of a club. . Find your club to join, the payment is ~$8 per month.. Club’s officers will explain you all the procedure.

Choose educational program

«Pathways» helps you learn communication and leadership skills that need practice. You can choose from 10 specialized learning projects, including: Presentation Mastery, Dynamic Leadership to name a few.

Deliver your first speech

Learn materials at the time convenient for you as «Pathways» is an online-based program. You will get all instructions and tips how to create good presentations. Book your first speech at the club’s meeting. Your fellow club members will give you evaluation and support throughout this journey.
